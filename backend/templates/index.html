Safety architecture

Medical Safety Notice

This app provides preliminary guidance only and is not a replacement for a qualified medical professional.

Emergency Symptoms

Chest pain, severe breathlessness, fainting, confusion, blue lips, very low SpO₂ or severe weakness should be treated as urgent.

AI Limitation

AI responses are informational and should not be treated as diagnosis, prescription, or guaranteed medical advice.

Consultation

For persistent, serious or worsening symptoms, consult a qualified healthcare professional promptly.
